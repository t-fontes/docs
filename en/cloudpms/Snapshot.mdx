## Snapshot - Check in

Total check-in's expected for today, the bar will indicate how many already processed

![](/images/cloudpms/snapshot/check_in.png)

Ex image: Already processed 38(80.85%) from a total of 42(100%) of pending check in's.

## Snapshot - Check Out

Total check-out's expected for today, the bar will indicate how many already processed

![](/images/cloudpms/snapshot/check_out.png)

Ex image: Already processed 640(98.46%) from a total of 650(100%) of pending check out's.

## Snapshot - Occupancy

Total occupied rooms and occupancy percentage from the available rooms

![](/images/cloudpms/snapshot/occupancy.png)

Ex image: Total of 173(31%) from total of 386(100%) of available rooms.

## Snapshot - Arrivals and Departures

Total scheduled arrivals and departures for the week.

![](/images/cloudpms/snapshot/arrivals_departures.png)

## Snapshot - Occupancy Weekly

Total percentage of reservations types for the week.

![](/images/cloudpms/snapshot/occupancy_weekly.png)

* **Total:** All reservations

* **Companies:** Reservations associated within an Agency/Company

* **Groups:** Reservations associated within a group

* **Individuals:** Reservations with no association to any Agency/Company

## Snapshot - Occupancy by room type

Total occupied/vacant and occupancy percentage by room type.
